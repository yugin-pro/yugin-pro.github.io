Rebuild GCP-based DWH to ClickHouse

Due to sanction pressure in 2022, keeping the DWH based on Google services was highly risky due to the potential for service blocks.

To overcome this challenge, I initiated a transition project where I served as the leader. I developed an architectural solution that gradually replaced the current data pipelines with new ones based on ClickHouse. Together with team managers, we broke the project into small tasks and created a realistic timeline to achieve the project objectives.

Converting Excel Reports to Power BI

Updating time consumes days, report data often do not match, continuous Excel file errors occur due to data processing limits, and improving reports takes months. These are typical problems of reporting built on Excel.

To address these issues while maintaining current reporting, I began converting the more problematic reports to the Power BI ecosystem. This approach preserved existing reports, improved maintenance, and created opportunities for future reporting development.
