Search Engine Marketing (SEM) / Pay-Per-Click (PPC)

  • Search Ads: Ads on search engine results pages (SERPs).
  • Display Ads: Visual ads on websites and apps within ad networks.
  • Shopping Ads: Product listings with price and image on SERPs.
  • Video Ads: Ads before, during, or after video content.
  • Remarketing/Retargeting: Ads to users who previously interacted with your website.

Social Media Marketing (SMM)

  • Organic Social Media: Community building and engagement without paid advertising, content creation and curation, community management, social listening, profile optimization.
  • Paid Social Media Advertising: Targeted ad campaigns on social media platforms, audience targeting, ad creative development, campaign management, performance analysis.

Content and Email Marketing

  • Blog Posts and Articles: Informative and engaging written content.
  • Ebooks and Whitepapers: In-depth, long-form content.
  • Infographics: Visual data presentation.
  • Videos: Engaging video content for various platforms.
  • Podcasts: Audio content for specific audiences.
  • Case Studies: Showcasing successful customer stories.
  • Email List Building: Growing a database of potential and current customers.
  • Email Segmentation: Targeted group division.
  • Email Campaign Creation: Designing and writing engaging email content.
  • Automation: Automated email sequences based on user behavior.
  • Performance Tracking: Analyzing email open rates, click-through rates, and conversions.

Affiliate and Partnership Marketing

  • Affiliate Marketing: Promoting products or services through affiliate networks, earning commissions on sales generated through your referral links.
  • Partnership Marketing: Collaborating with other businesses or influencers to promote each other's products or services, leveraging each other's audiences for mutual benefit.
  • Influencer Marketing: Partnering with influencers to promote your brand, products, or services to their followers.
  • Performance Tracking: Monitoring the success of affiliate and partnership campaigns through metrics such as clicks, conversions, and ROI.
  • Relationship Management: Building and maintaining strong relationships with affiliates and partners to ensure long-term success.
