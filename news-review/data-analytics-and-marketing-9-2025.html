The shift from ads to influence: What marketers need to know after DMWF Asia day 2

marketingtechnews

Influencer marketing's evolution from simple endorsements to genuine storytelling, coupled with the rising prominence of employee-generated content, underscores the consumer shift towards trust over traditional advertising. To maximize social media reach, brands are leveraging organic content testing and strategically boosting high-performing posts, while also refining influencer selection through in-depth audience analysis beyond surface-level metrics. Measuring campaign success now involves robust tracking methods like UTM links and pixel integration, moving beyond vanity metrics to demonstrate tangible ROI. Looking ahead to 2025, the focus remains on embracing creator trust, utilizing precise data, and ensuring content resonates authentically, signaling a permanent shift in how brands engage with their audiences.

Measuring ROI, sales, or revenue alone cannot fully capture the success of an influencer campaign. Seasoned marketers understand that each strategic move and tool has its own boundaries, implementation limits, and success metrics. This is why measuring gained customer experience is becoming more than just "measuring success"—it's about understanding how to shape strategy using the knowledge gained about user experience. Boosting your reach and performance with Google Display Ads

google-ads-commerce

To effectively engage today's consumers across a fragmented digital landscape, Google Display Ads (GDA) has expanded its reach and enhanced its creative capabilities. By integrating new inventory across diverse platforms like CTV and adding GDA to Demand Gen campaigns, advertisers can now connect with audiences on their preferred channels, driving increased conversions. Furthermore, leveraging advanced AI models like Imagen 3, GDA simplifies creative generation, facilitates team collaboration through asset preview galleries, and ensures brand consistency with customizable templates. Coupled with strengthened quality standards and fraud protections, GDA empowers advertisers to maximize reach and performance while maintaining brand integrity, fostering meaningful connections with customers.

How WeWard Boosted Engagement with Amplitude

amplitude

WeWard, an app rewarding users for walking, leverages Amplitude analytics to understand and enhance user engagement. By focusing on the initial onboarding experience, they clarified the step validation process, leading to a 10.5% increase in activated users. To maintain motivation, they introduced "joker" features and milestone rewards for streaks, resulting in a 14% rise in users achieving seven-day streaks. These data-driven improvements, facilitated by Amplitude, have contributed to a 24% increase in average user walking activity, demonstrating the app's success in promoting healthier lifestyles.
