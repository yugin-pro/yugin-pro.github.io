The business impacts of data quality issues—what they look like and how to think about fixes

mixpanel

Common problems with data

  • Over tracking
  • Data silos
  • Incomplete data
  • Inconsistent data
  • Duplicate data
  • Inaccurate data
  • Sequencing issues
  • Misconfiguration issues

Failure to acknowledge the importance of data and data quality in your business strategy leads to lack of ownership, processes, documentation and/or tools, which leads to data quality issues.

Many of these issues arise due to natural business reasons such as shared responsibility for data collection and storage processes between numerous teams, tight deadlines and technical debts, legacy code bases and tech solutions, and rapidly changing priorities.

Need to Know: How big data plus people panels improves data quality

nielsen

Learn more about combining data sources to increase accuracy

...We measured how this new Big Data + Panel calibration method performed against the existing panel-based currency in the New York designated market area (DMA) in May 2023 and found that the average relative error for audience estimates in that market decreased anywhere between 10% (for the early fringe daypart) and 25% (for primetime and late fringe).

It is important to understand that digital data often represents samples and technical information. For instance, what we call users are usually devices or even browsers, each with its own limitations. To decrease the chances of making inaccurate decisions, it is essential to combine information from different sources, whether it is data from a website or an application.

How to Measure True ROAS in Ecommerce: Amplitude + Shopify

amplitude

Case Study: How to Measure ROAS Using Amplitude, Shopify, and Google Sheets. This case study explains a step-by-step plan to calculate ROAS, including vital stages such as:

  • Understanding and mapping out the user journey to identify key touchpoints.
  • Implementing methods to integrate data from Ads sources, Amplitude, Shopify, and Google Sheets.
  • Establishing consistent tracking mechanisms to ensure data accuracy.
  • Aligning definitions of key metrics across platforms to maintain consistency in reporting.

I would like to highlight that what may seem like a simple architecture to implement at first glance can turn out to be unscalable and cost-consuming as the business evolves. I would recommend considering more stable and scalable architectures to implement.

From Data to Delight: Why Hyper-Personalization Is the Future of Business

salesforce

Hyper-personalization relies on a mix of:

Behavioral data: How users interact with your website or app Transactional data: Purchase history, loyalty rewards, return history, reviews, and payment preferences Contextual data: Time, location of the customer, and the type of device they are using Social data: Likes, shares, comments, and interests across social platforms

Absolutely resonates with my experience and knowledge. Learning more about behavior patterns and user habits contributes significantly to the success of developing new products or features, cost-efficient promotions, building trust, and brand loyalty.
